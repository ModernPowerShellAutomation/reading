The American philosopher of New Zealand origin Annette Baier6, who dedicated his entire career to the study of the "female voice", as she says, of David Hume and her moral implications, proposes to displace morality based on abstract principles and the ideal of justice, for an ethic that emerges from the treatment and the reciprocity game that make up the empirical and plural basis of concrete social life.

En esta ética la virtud principal es la confianza, como concepto puente entre la simpatía y la reflexión, el amor y el deber, el sentimiento y la razón.

In this ethic the main virtue is trust, as a bridge concept between sympathy and reflection, love and duty, feeling and reason.

Dios ya había sido asesinado antes.

God had already been killed before.

Entonces empezó a ser metódicamente ejecutada, también, la humanidad y numerosos animales y ecosistemas.

Then it began to be methodically executed, also, humanity and numerous animals and ecosystems.

At the same time, the twentieth century is the century of demographic running.

Scheduled annihilation and overpopulation: the two sides of a paradox that is advertised under the imminent sign of the catastrophe.

El siglo XX es, por ello, el tiempo del fin de las esperanzas de la humanidad en el desarrollo como base del progreso ético y político.

The twentieth century is, therefore, the time of the end of humanity's hopes in development as the basis of ethical and political progress.

Lo que ya anunciaban ilustrados inquietos como Rousseau o Schiller en el siglo XVIII, que a medida que el conocimiento y la técnica avanzaban, la virtud retrocedía, se hacía evidencia en la muerte masiva y continua de millones de personas, en la desigualdad y en la injusticia social.

What they already announced restless illustrated such as Rousseau or Schiller in the 18th century, which as knowledge and technique advanced, virtue retreated, evidence was made in the massive and continuous death of millions of people, in inequality and social injustice.

La filosofía del siglo XX es, obviamente, la expresión polifónica de esta trágica experiencia histórica.

The philosophy of the twentieth century is, obviously, the polyphonic expression of this tragic historical experience.

But because it is philosophy, it is not just its reflection.

Implica también abrir sus problemas de tal manera que puedan ser atravesados.

It also implies opening their problems in such a way that they can be crossed.

Una mirada sobre la filosofía del siglo XX ofrece una impresión inesperada y sorprendente: el siglo de la muerte administrada tiene una riqueza de pensamiento inagotable.

A look at the philosophy of the twentieth century offers an unexpected and surprising impression: the century of administered death has a wealth of inexhaustible thought.

De una crudeza dolorosa, es inseparablemente un caleidoscopio de una creatividad asombrosa.

Of a painful rawness, it is inseparably a kaleidoscope of amazing creativity.

La herida del presente exige ir más allá sin negar su profundidad.

The wound of the present demands to go further without denying its depth.

Therefore, all the philosophies of this last century are invitations to look for new paths, to resume missing, to think otherwise, to invent new concepts, to stop being what we are to go to meet what we do not know, to create other possibilities of life, to listen to the unexpected, to think about movement and encounter, to welcome what is rejected, what is expelled and what was denied by our deadly civilization.

Son filosofías que aceptan la finitud del pensamiento, la fragmentación de la experiencia, lo que hay y no podemos decir, lo que escapa y vuelve a nosotros.

They are philosophies that accept the finitude of thought, the fragmentation of the experience, what there is and we cannot say, what escapes and returns to us.

Nietzsche and the philosophy of suspicion The twentieth century is the century that started late and that we still do not know if it is over.

En el campo de la filosofía, el siglo XX desarrolló las consecuencias del giro radical que se había dado en el siglo anterior.

In the field of philosophy, the twentieth century developed the consequences of the radical turn that had occurred in the previous century.

Este giro puede ser explicado de diversas maneras: como crisis del mundo cristiano-burgués, como cuestionamiento de los ideales de la Ilustración o como disolución del sistema hegeliano y del horizonte de sistematicidad y totalidad del pensamiento filosófico.

This turn can be explained in various ways: as a crisis in the Christian-Burgués world, as questioning of the ideals of the Enlightenment or as dissolution of the Hegelian system and the horizon of systematicity and totality of philosophical thought.

Son interpretaciones diversas de una misma ruptura histórica que, si bien son ciertas, todas ellas ponen el acento en el fracaso y en la clausura de una etapa del pensamiento y de la civilización occidentales.

They are diverse interpretations of the same historical rupture that, although they are true, all of them emphasize the failure and closing of a stage of Western thought and civilization.

Desde ahí, la filosofía contemporánea sólo puede ser leída como expresión de esa crisis.

From there, contemporary philosophy can only be read as an expression of that crisis.

Sin embargo, todos estos cambios, crisis y rupturas pueden ser interpretados también como la aparición de un nuevo modo de preguntar por la verdad que redefine el espacio del pensamiento.

However, all these changes, crises and ruptures can also be interpreted as the appearance of a new way of asking about the truth that redefines the space of thought.

Este nuevo modo de preguntar por la verdad no es el efecto de un corte ni es la mera sustitución de un modelo de pensamiento por otro.

This new way of asking about the truth is not the effect of a cut nor is the mere replacement of one model of thought by another.
