Lack of robustness.

Los algoritmos se derivan de asociaciones, y dado que no entendemos cuáles son los procesos subyacentes, pueden ser muy sensibles a los cambios.

Algorithms are derived from associations, and since we do not understand what the underlying processes are, they can be very sensitive to changes.

Aunque solo nos importe la precisión, y no tanto la verdad científica, tenemos que seguir recordando los principios básicos del ciclo PPDAC, y los estadios que hay que seguir, desde los datos obtenidos de una muestra hasta las afirmaciones sobre la población objetivo.

Although we only care about precision, and not so much scientific truth, we have to continue remembering the basic principles of the PPDAC cycle, and the stadiums that must be followed, from the data obtained from a sample to the statements about the target population.

Para los análisis predictivos, esta población objetivo incluye casos futuros, y si nada cambia, entonces unos algoritmos construidos sobre datos del pasado deberían funcionar bien.

For predictive analyzes, this target population includes future cases, and if nothing changes, then algorithms built on past data should work well.

This algorithm in principle worked well, but then, in 2013, it began to dramatically overcome the infection rates of the flu: an explanation is that the changes introduced by Google in the search engine could have generated more search terms referred to the flu.
